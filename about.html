About ATCON Construction Ltd

ATCON Construction Ltd is a professional Architectural Firm, Structural Engineering Firm, and full-service Construction Company specializing in residential and commercial building solutions across Nairobi and Kenya.

We deliver durable, modern, and high-quality construction projects backed by expert engineering, efficient project management, and strong client partnerships. Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to technical excellence and lasting value.

With years of hands-on experience, our team focuses on delivering projects that meet structural integrity standards, cost efficiency, and timely completion — without compromise.

Our lead engineer, Dennis Munene, P.E., brings over a decade of field and design experience, ensuring every structure we produce is safe, functional, and beautifully executed.
